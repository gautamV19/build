About Us

We, at the IIT Madras, are working towards making logistics operations more efficient and sustainable by using state-of-the-art algorithms and technologies.
Having built route optimizing, vehicle allocation, ride/space sharing, orienteering algorithms for the last 4 years, we have integrated all into a SaaS software service. We look to improve the efficiency of our algorithms and add a lot more features in the coming month.
You can join us if this field seems interesting to you.
